The Best Movies According to Sreeja Gardas

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Salaar Part 1: Ceasefire

Salaar Part 1: Ceasefire draws you in with its gripping action and the sheer intensity Prabhas brings to his role,
making every moment on screen a thrilling experience.

Hi Nanna

Hi Nanna is its heartwarming portrayal of relationships, especially the bond between a father and daughter, which is
filled with emotional depth, innocence, and love that can resonate with anyone

Robo

Robo is its innovative storytelling that blends cutting-edge technology with human emotions.
